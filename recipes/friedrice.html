Fried Rice

DESCRIPTION

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until tender.
  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and crack the eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs until fully cooked.
  4. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and mix everything together.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil (if using), and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.

Notes

You can add cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu for extra protein. Feel free to customize the vegetables based on your preference.

Enjoy your meal!
